### About Me

I'm a technical professional with 10+ years across software engineering, SaaS platform support, and data.
Most recently I worked at Dimagi on CommCare, supporting enterprise deployments for global public health organizations.
My day-to-day there was troubleshooting APIs and integrations, digging through logs and SQL, reproducing bugs, and turning messy technical problems into clear fixes and better documentation.
Before that I spent 6+ years building and shipping web and mobile applications with PHP (Laravel), Python (Django), and React, growing from developer to technical lead.

I'm strongest at the intersection of engineering and customers.
I can read the code, query the database, and then explain the problem in plain language to the person affected by it.

### AI & Automation 🤖

I'm an AI enthusiast focused on practical, applied use rather than hype.
At Dimagi I led the implementation of an AI-powered ticket routing system that cut average resolution time significantly and measurably improved customer satisfaction.
I also built workflow automations connecting Zapier, Jira, and Google Sheets to remove repetitive manual work from support operations.
I'm AWS AI Practitioner certified and completed an AI Engineering course, and I keep experimenting with LLM APIs and automation workflows in personal projects.
My interest is simple: use AI to make support, operations, and data work faster and less painful for real teams.
